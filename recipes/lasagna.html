Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna is made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and plenty of cheese. It's the ultimate comfort food that will satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a skillet and add tomato sauce.
  3. Layer the cooked noodles, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
