Spaghetti

Description

This simple spaghetti recipe features al dente pasta tossed in a classic tomato sauce and garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese. It's a quick and delicious meal that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the tomato sauce in a separate pan over medium heat.
  3. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the pan with the tomato sauce. Toss to coat.
  4. Serve the spaghetti hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese.
